## My Love

From the moment I first saw you, my world has been brighter than I ever could've imagined. You brought colors to my life that I didn't even know existed, and every day since has been a journey of discovering how deepl I care for you. My love for you runs deeper than the ocean, streching far beyond the horizon where the sky kisses the sea, endless and unwavering

## My Apology

I am truly sorry for everything I've done that may have hurt you or made you feel ignored, unheard or unimportant. I know there have been moments when my words or actions didn't reflect the love I feel for you, and for that, I am deeply regretful. Please undertand that it was never my intention to make you feel less than cherished. I know I keep saying the same words "I am sorry" over and over, and I understand that it may sound repetitive or even empty at times. When I say that every apology comes from the bottom of my heart. 

Time may ebb and flow, and life may pull us in different directions, but my devotion to you remains an eternal current, always  bringing me back to you. Even when we drift or far apart, my heart holds you close, and every beat carries the hope of making things right. 

> Love is like the ocean, deep and ever-moving,
Sometimes calm, sometimes stormy, yet always alive.
I feel the pull of your waves, the warmth of your tides,
Even when currents clash, I cannot stop being drawn to you.

## You 

You are my horizon, my calm in every storm, my deepest joy, and my greatest treasure. I may keep repeating my apologies, but it is only because I cannot bear the though of hurting or losing the precious bond we share. I hope with time, my actions can show youy the sincerity of my words and the depth of my love, because nothing in this world matters more to me than you.

>Love is like the ocean, endless and deep,
Sometimes whispering softly, sometimes roaring in leaps.
I feel the pull of your waves, the warmth of your tides,
And I want to be with you too, where our hearts collide.
